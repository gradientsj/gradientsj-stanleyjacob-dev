Systems

Design walkthroughs of the systems behind familiar products, written the way the conversation actually runs in an interview. Each one starts by agreeing on scope, turns vague scale into concrete numbers, sketches the architecture with a diagram, and then goes deep on the parts where the hard decisions live, closing with the follow-up questions an interviewer tends to ask. The designs range from classic warm-ups like a URL shortener to payment systems, stock exchanges, and the machine learning systems behind search and recommendations.
