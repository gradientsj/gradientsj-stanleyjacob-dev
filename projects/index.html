Uptime & SLO Monitor

Probes a set of live public & personal endpoints on a schedule, records latency and availability in Postgres, and serves this public status page. It computes availability/latency SLIs over rolling windows, tracks error-budget burn against per-service SLO targets, and fires multi-window multi-burn-rate alerts. Exposes Prometheus metrics (with Grafana dashboards), is packaged with Docker, and ships Terraform + Helm for a GKE deployment.
