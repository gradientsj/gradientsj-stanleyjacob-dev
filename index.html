About

My focus is machine learning. I've built models across vision, audio, and language, from small networks up to large transformers, and I care just as much about getting them to run well in production as I do about training them.

That foundation comes from electrical engineering at UT Austin and three years of computer science at Stanford, where I focused on machine learning, generative models, and the systems that make them fast. Since then I've worked at both large companies and startups, building ML and software systems end to end.

Outside of work I like puzzles and building small projects. If you'd like to talk or work together, feel free to reach out.
